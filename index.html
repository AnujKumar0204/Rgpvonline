About University

Rgpv (Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya) is a Technological University of the state of Madhya Pradesh (M.P) & all Engineering & Diploma Colleges & Pharmacy Degree are affiliated to this university.Rgtu established in the year 1998, by Madhya Pradesh Vidhan Sabha Act 13, 1998. The campus of Rajiv Gandhi Technological University is around 241.14 acres in area situated on four lanes Risen - Narsingarh by pass main highway. Rgpv offers a lots of courses such as Engineering, M.Tech, Diploma, Pharmacy, Management, polytechnic etc. In university the number of students are enrolled in the university, having some 270,000 students. Under this university there are numbers of institutions are come such as LNCT, Oriental, Truba, Bansal, TIT, career college bhopal and nearly 200 affiliated Engineering Colleges in bhopal and overall MP. And the vision of next five years is interventions in four main areas. The first is All Weather Approach Road. And second is Promote use of IT in technical education through promotion of e-Learning and web enabled teaching processes. And third is Enhance opportunities for postgraduate education and research in areas of high relevance to nations science and technology capabilities. And fourth is Providing effective mechanisms and support facilities for developing faculty and learning resources for its affiliated institutions.
